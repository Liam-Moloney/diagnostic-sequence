Diagnostic Sequence

A documented case study examining how diagnostic conclusions can become administratively fixed before clinical inquiry is complete.

The Story Timeline Documents Analysis

Introduction

This project documents the sequence of events surrounding a neurological diagnosis made in 2017 and the institutional processes that followed.

The materials presented here examine how diagnostic conclusions can become administratively fixed while key elements of clinical inquiry remain unresolved.

The project presents primary records, a chronological reconstruction of events, and a structural analysis of the diagnostic process.

How to Read This Site

Readers encountering the case for the first time should begin with The Story, which provides a narrative overview of events.

The Timeline presents a chronological reconstruction from early symptoms through the present.

The Documents section contains the primary records forming the evidentiary basis of the case.

The Analysis section examines the structural issue of diagnostic sequence and administrative finality.
