Introduction

This project documents the sequence of events surrounding a neurological diagnosis made in 2017 and the institutional processes that followed.

The materials presented here examine how diagnostic conclusions can become administratively fixed while key elements of clinical inquiry remain unresolved.

The project presents primary records, a chronological reconstruction of events, and a structural analysis of the diagnostic process.
