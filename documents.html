Primary Records

GP Referral — 29 June 2017

Urgent referral from general practice to neurology describing abnormal gait and severe bilateral foot symptoms. This document establishes the presenting clinical problem at the point of entry into specialist care.

Neurology Discharge Summary — 11 July 2017

Hospital discharge document recording the diagnosis of idiopathic Parkinson’s disease and outlining the clinical pathway following admission.

Long Term Illness Certification — 9 July 2017

Statutory application certifying Parkinson’s disease under the Irish Long Term Illness scheme, creating administrative recognition of the diagnosis.

DAT Scan Addendum — 13 July 2017

Correspondence reporting the results of dopamine transporter imaging carried out shortly after discharge.

Later Clinical Records

Clinical Correspondence (2017–2025)

Follow-up neurology correspondence documenting treatment adjustments and ongoing clinical management under the Parkinson’s diagnostic framework.

Genetic Confirmation Letter — February 2026

Neurology correspondence confirming pathogenic variants in the PRKN gene, establishing a monogenic cause of Parkinsonism and dystonia.
